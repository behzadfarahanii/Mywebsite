Research Interests

Research interests is in Autonomous Systems, Machine Learning, and Robotics focus on developing intelligent systems that can operate independently in various domains, Autonomous Systems involve designing algorithms and control systems for machines that can perform tasks and make decisions autonomously, Also, enhancing perception, decision-making, planning, and control mechanisms for higher autonomy and adaptability is a primary research goal, and ensuring real-time performance and the safety of the control algorithm is crucial to prevent discomfort or injury to users.

Furthermore, I would like to research developing algorithms and models that enable systems to become safe and resilient in unknown environments. Specific research topics include safe reinforcement learning for autonomous vehicles, multi-robot systems, safe and resilient systems. I am interested in developing machine learning algorithms using the verification of neural networks and formal methods techniques that integrate sensor data, enable learning-based decision-making, coordinate multiple robots, ensure transparency in decision processes. Overall, the research in these fields aims to advance the capabilities and intelligence of autonomous systems for effective and safe operation in real-world.

Biography

My name is Behzad Farahani, and I was born in Tehran, Iran. My passions lie in chess, strategic games, and swimming. I have always been fascinated with mathematics and strategic thinking. I am learning courses such as mathematics, physics, and chemistry, which have resulted in the average GPA was 17.5 out of 20. This academic success has set the stage for my future pursuits. In September 2012, I embarked on my undergraduate journey by enrolling at Sharif University of Technology's International Campus in Tehran. I pursued a B.Sc degree in the Mechatronics Engineering program. Recognizing the importance of knowledge, creativity, and implementation, I approached my studies with enthusiasm and a strong desire to make a positive impact on society.

During my time at the university, I not only focused on my own education but also took on the role of an assistant teacher at the Strength of Materials Laboratory. Additionally, I joined the Robotics team in RoboCup 2014 under the supervision of Professor Hemmatyar. My ultimate goal is to leverage my knowledge and skills to make significant contributions to the field of Autonomous Systems and reinforcement learning. With a strong foundation in mathematics and a passion for creative problem-solving, I aspire to enhance the safety and resilience of robotics in unknown environments.
